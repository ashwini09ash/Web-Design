SANJEEVINI

What is Sanjeevini?
In Hindu mythology, Sanjeevani is a magical herb which has the power to cure any malady. Herb is mentioned in the Ramayana when, Ravana’s son Indrajit hurls a powerful weapon at Lakshmana.

What is project Sanjeevini?
Sanjeevini is a short term project by Relief Call which aims to provide medical aid to underprivileged CKD patients who are on Haemodialysis.

Why project Sanjeevini is started?
With the success of Relief Call’s Dialysis program we also aimed at providing medical aid to underprivileged patients by providing important life saving medicines. One of our contributors joined their hands with us and thus helped us creating a wonderful short term project called ‘Sanjeevini’.

Benefits of Sanjeevini.

  1. Sanjeevini is a short term project.
  2. Duration of the project is planned for 5 – 10 months.
  3. Project starts from 01-Oct-2015.
  4. Project Sanjeevini aims at providing medical aid.
  5. Medical aid is by providing any or all of the below mentioned medicines for free.
  6. Medicine includes Insulin, insulin syringes, HTN tablets, Vitamin tablets, hepatitis B vaccine, and other important medicines.
  7. Medicine list should be prescribed only by consultant Nephrologist/ Hospital where dialysis is carried out.
  8. Sanjeevini is not a subsidized program and aims at providing medicines for free.

Beneficiaries of Sanjeevini

  1. Only patients registered with Relief Call are eligible.
  2. Patients choosen for Relief Call dialysis program are giving high priority.
  3. Sanjeevini is a short term project and permanent medical aid is not provided.
  4. Sanjeevini is limited to only Mysore region.
  5. Patients will be intimidated before termination/ end of Sanjeevini project.
