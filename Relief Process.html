RELIEF CALL PROCESS


  • The first step at Relief is to Identify CKD patients who are under Dialysis. Patient are identified in two ways,
    1. Online Registration : Here, friend of a patient or relative of a patient or a patient him(her)self register with us by visiting our “Register a patient” page and fill up the required details.
    2. Offline Registration : Here, volunteers would collect information of affected patients in Mysuru by personally meeting them at hospitals.
  • In either case, a strict backgound verification would be done by the volunteers for the authenticity of data.
  • Relief Call then filter the CKD patients who are at a lower economic conditions and who are not covered under any medical insurance/Governmet Schemes in the background verification process.
  • Relief Call choses certain number of patients who are really in need of help after background verification and the number would solely depend on the funds that is raised at Relief.
  • Financial help provided by Relief Call after core team approval and is only for Dialysis. No other treatment/medicines/consultation/hospitalisation fee is provided by Relief Call.
