SAMSHUDDHI

What is Samshuddhi?
Samshuddhi is a word from Sanskrit origin, which means Purity or Cleanliness.

What is project Samshuddhi?
Samshuddhi is a project by Relief Call which aims to provide financial help to underprivileged CKD patients who are on Haemodialysis, through subsidized dialysis after a thorough background check.

Why project Samshuddhi is started?
Kidney failure or CKD is one such disease where the treatment is really not affordable, one has to ideally get dialysed thrice a week which counts up to 12 dialysis per month. We realized that there are so many out there who really can’t afford a prescribed number of dialysis per week due to financial problems, then a toddler called ‘Relief Call’ came into existence

Benefits of Samshuddhi.

  1. Samshuddhi is a long term project.
  2. Project starts from 01-Aug-2015.
  3. Project Samshuddhi aims at providing Finacial aid.
  4. Finacial aid is provided as a subsidized dialysis, after a thorough background check.
  5. Helping hands for needy patients by supporting them finacially and in turn mentally.
  6. Kind Support from Brindavan Hospital and their doctors.

Beneficiaries of Samshuddhi

  1. Only patients registered with Relief Call are eligible.
  2. Patients registered with Relief Call dialysis program undergo thorough background check about their family income, medical insurance and other existing finacial Supports.
  3. Patients choosen for Samshuddhi, provided with 29% subsidy for every dialysis cost.
  4. Samshuddhi is limited to only Mysore region.
  5. Currenlty our organization is working with Brindavan Hospital.
  6. Patients will be intimidated before termination/ end of Samshuddhi project.
